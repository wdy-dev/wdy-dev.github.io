Hello World

July 12, 2023 · 3 min

Welcome to my blog#

First off, I am human who occasionally wonders if our world might be more akin to a computer program than we realize. Don’t be alarmed, it’s merely a philosophical pondering, not a declaration of eccentricity. I can imagine a day when AI might start write its own blog. Yet, let’s not stray too far into the AI. The matter at hand is my own foray into blogging, inspired by Lilian Weng’s blog. After spending some time writing on my own, I realized the need for a platform to share my thoughts.

One key motivation for starting this blog is to push myself to think and write more consistently. In the age of LLM, the significance of writing becomes even more pronounced. While you can rely on LLM for generating text, it cannot replace the power of human thinking. Language abilities should not be mistaken for thinking abilities, as emphasized by Yann LeCun.

I plan to cover a variety of topics that interest me. To give it a fun AI spin, I like to think of it as ‘decoding myself’, where each post is a snapshot of my thought process. We’ll be exploring the world of machine learning, as well as pulling back the curtain on my personal experiences in the tech industry, with focus on things like Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Large Language Models (LLM).

You might also see occasional discussions about startup ideas and investment, but none of the content shared should be taken as financial advice, as I’m still climbing the success ladder myself!

I aim to update my blog at least once a week, as I find setting ambitious goals helps to keep me motivated and productive. However, I am also mindful of maintaining quality content, so editing and occasionally deleting posts that do not meet my standards may occur from time to time.

Stay healthy. Think more. Write more.

Technical Insights#

I managed to have the site up and fully functional in roughly four hours. This time-frame encompassed a variety of tasks, including:

  1. Experimenting with Jekyll Themes, although I wasn’t able to locate a minimalist theme equipped with an integrated search feature.
  2. Transitioning to Hugo and implementing based on the Papermod theme.
  3. Adjusting GitHub username to reflect my real name, owing to the platform’s restriction of hosting only ‘username.github.io’.
  4. Committing the built directory, as opposed to the Hugo source.
